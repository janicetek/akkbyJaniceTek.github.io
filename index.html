About Us

A.K.K. Engineering Sdn. Bhd. is led by experienced professionals, emphasizes quality and service. It holds G7 contractor registration with CIDB Malaysia, ISO 9001:2008 certification, and authorization as a Pressure Vessel manufacturer licensed by ASME and NB. Registered with JKKP, A.K.K. Engineering is competent in pressure vessel manufacturing. The company has participated in various industries nationwide and expanded internationally since 2007, completing over RM 250 million worth of projects. In 2015, subsidiaries were established in Borneo and Singapore, diversifying into construction material supply and logistics. A.K.K. Group aims for steady growth, aligning with its vision for the future.
